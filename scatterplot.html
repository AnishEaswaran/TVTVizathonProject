Scatterplot

The scatterplot provides datapoints for each nation on its absolute change in HDI and absolute change in HFI. Hovering over the points reveals that information as well as the ratio of absolute change in HDI over absolute change in HFI. When the linear regression was conducted before creating this scatterplot, a p value of 0.001775 was generated, revealing a statistically significant relationship between the variables. 62% of the nations had a positive linear correlation between a change in human freedom and a change in the human development index, meaning nearly 4.75 billon people worldwide are vulnerable to declines in development levels due to recent restrictions on freedom.
